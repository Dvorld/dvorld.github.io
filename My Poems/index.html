    Me and My Poems.

    Hello!

    The joy of writing is something very Serene. For the freedom of beautifying it with words is Mine. The art that completes Me. The bliss of experiencing my meet up with Self. Incredible, a feeling, when I fall in love with myself at the end of every writing.

    Every time I read the lines above, that I wrote, indeed brings to me, a sense of completeness and drives me to pen down my thoughts. Simple little episodes happening daily around me, have always been the basis of my write-ups. I endeavor to weave the flow of such thoughts through simple words with a hope to bring a pause and smile to my readers in their busy ongoing life on a positive note, hence convincing myself of serving the purpose of my existence.

    An adorer of nature and relationships, I also attempted to unveil my perspective towards it through few of my writings on nature, motherhood and friendship. My insights about a couple of books that became a turning point in my life, as well are part of my writings.

    Having said that, a heartfelt thanks to every reader for taking time to read my blog and of course, to the most worthwhile comments.

    Happie Reading. 🙂

    By the Riverside

    By the Riverside, seeking solitude.. With the sense of gratitude.

    To breathe in the fragrance of Nature.. That becomes a complete rejuvenator.

    To hear the tweet of birds.. And pen down some pretty words.

    To see them fly hither-thither.. All happy around their mother.

    To admire the sky clear hued.. Lying in the arms of greens and wood.

    While the stream is flowing by.. The energy is eternally high.

    Happiness is in the air.. And the life looks so very fair.

    By the Riverside, seeking solitude.. With the sense of gratitude.

    -Dhara 23-09-2021

    For Vijay

    I asked him for a room, and he made me a Palace, thus, he made me his Princess!

    He has handled the worst of me. He has handled the craziest of me. He has got more than what I have ever asked for.

    I always complained and moved on thinking he never understood me. But, he always worked on each of those complaints behind the scenes. A few, I even forgot, that I had complained.

    We have laughed, we have fought, we have had fun, we have faced challenges, But all this, we have done together.

    I might have given upon myself, but he has never allowed me to let down, he has never ever given upon me.

    I cannot thank him enough, for what he has been and what he has made me. I cannot thank him enough, for bearing all my moods, regardless of what mood he might have been in I cannot thank him enough, for giving me the freedom,which, when I sit back and realize, no one could have ever given me. I cannot thank him enough for just not giving up on me.

    I asked him for a room, and he made me a Palace, thus, he made me his Princess!
